About Our Studio

At DESIGN RESPONSE, we believe architecture is more than just buildings; it's about crafting environments that enrich lives. With a diverse portfolio encompassing residential, commercial, institutional, and hospitality projects, as well as urban design, exhibition spaces, interiors, and landscaping, we are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of architectural excellence.

Led by architects, Udayan and Akshay, our firm brings together a passionate team of over 15 in-house professionals, including architects, civil engineers, and site engineers. Supported by a network of over 20 consultants and associates, we collaborate closely to realise our vision of sustainable, contextually sensitive design.

At DESIGN RESPONSE, every project is a testament to our commitment to innovation and client-centric solutions. Whether creating timeless residences or dynamic commercial spaces, we integrate cutting-edge design with a deep understanding of local context and environmental sustainability. Explore our portfolio and discover how we transform ideas into extraordinary built environments.

Our Philosophy

At DESIGN RESPONSE, architecture is not just about creating structures; it's about shaping experiences and fostering connections. We believe in the power of design to transform lives and elevate communities. Our philosophy is rooted in three core principles:

  • Innovation: We embrace innovation as a catalyst for creating spaces that are not only functional but also inspirational. Through continuous exploration and experimentation, we push the boundaries of architectural possibility.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to sustainable design practices that minimise environmental impact and maximise efficiency. From energy-efficient buildings to eco-friendly materials, we integrate sustainability into every aspect of our projects.
  • Contextual Sensitivity: Understanding the unique context of each project is fundamental to our approach. By respecting local culture, history, and environment, we create architecture that harmonises seamlessly with its surroundings.

Principle Architects

Udayan

Udayan is a visionary architect and the Director of Design Response, with an impressive career spanning over 28 years in the field of architecture. An alumnus of IIT Roorkee and GCA Lucknow, Udayan has consistently pushed the boundaries of design and innovation. His deep-rooted expertise in hospitality and large-scale residential developments has earned him a distinguished reputation across Uttarakhand and beyond.
Over the years, Udayan has successfully led the design and execution of numerous high-profile projects, including luxury 5-star hotels, institutional buildings, and expansive group housing projects. His work exemplifies a harmonious blend of aesthetics, functionality, and sustainability, ensuring that each project enhances the built environment while meeting client and user needs.
With a commitment to delivering excellence, Udayan continues to shape the architectural landscape, creating spaces that inspire and enrich the lives of their inhabitants.

Akshay

Akshay Agarwal is an architect and Director at Design Response, with a decade of experience in the field. A graduate of GCA Lucknow, Akshay has a passion for cultural heritage and modern design, which is reflected in his innovative approach to architecture. He further honed his skills by participating in a prestigious summer school at Universität Tübingen, Germany, where he explored global perspectives on Smart cities.

In addition to his architectural expertise, Akshay is a co-author of the book "Walking with Laata," which delves into the rich culture and heritage of the Doon Valley. His dedication to preserving the historical essence of the region, while integrating contemporary design practices, sets him apart as a thought leader in the field.

At Design Response, Akshay combines his love for heritage with his forward-thinking design approach, ensuring that each project not only meets functional needs but also resonates with the cultural context of its environment. His work continues to contribute to the evolving architectural narrative of Uttarakhand and beyond.
